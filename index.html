Case Study 01

Pakistan Fashion eCommerce Brand

Overview

A Pakistan-based fashion eCommerce brand selling clothing through its online store, aiming to scale revenue profitably through Meta Ads.

The Challenge

Modest Fashion Brand — Abayas & Women's Clothing
